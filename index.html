Relationships between health risks and economic indices

Poverty

We found that there is a positive relationship between poverty and health risks. People with the higher poverty level have higher risks to get obeses, smoke more, and cannot afford the healthcare.

Age

Most people in our dataset are between 34 and 44 years old. There is no significant relationship between age and health risks; we could only tell that how many percentages are obeses, smoking, and lacking healthcare in this age range.

Household Income

There is a nagative relationship between household income and health risks. People with higher income level are less likely to get obese, smoke, and lack healthcare.
